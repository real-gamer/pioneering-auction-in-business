Liquidation Online Auction Offers Items Worth $1,931,571 In Value

Local auction house Arthur & Sons Ltd are proud to announce this year’s annual liquidation auction 2020.

With over 30+ years experience in the industry, Arthur & Sons has been a pioneering auction in business, starting with just 2 employees and now employing over 65 staff members.

We pride ourselves on building a stock room of items purchased from liquidation sales and offering them on display for purchase by the public.

Every year, over 1,000 customers wait in anticipation for our annual liquidation auction as they know great deals will be on offer!

Our auctions focus primarily on furniture, overbought stock, electronics, office equipment and more!

There truly is something for everyone.

Last year, we were pleased to host our auction online for the first time in company history, making our auctions available to the public spanning across Canada.

More than $570,000 worth of valuable items were purchased online in a single day, and this year, we’re hoping to double that!
